Change email address

Update the email address associated with your account.

Quick tip

Head over to your Account Settings now to update your email address.

Open your Account Settings
  1. From the top corner of the homepage, click the ⚙ gear icon to go to your Account Settings.
  2. On the Account tab, click on Edit across your email address.
  3. Enter your account password, and click Confirm password.
  4. Once the field is unlocked, enter a new email address. It should not be linked to an existing Canva account.
  5. Click Save changes.

A confirmation email will be sent to the new email address. Check it, and click Confirm email address to confirm the email address update.

Can't change email address

To successfully change your account email, use an email address that isn’t associated with an existing Canva account. If you get an error message saying the email address you want to use is already registered, here’s what you can do: Use a different email address OR Check if you can access the account registered to your preferred email address If you can access it, change its email address to a different one to unlink your preferred email address, so you can use it in the other account. If you can’t access it, you can request for the account with your preferred email address to be deleted by filling out this form . Note that this is irreversible, and doing so will delete all designs created in the account. If you still need help after doing the steps above, click here to contact our Support Team.

Can’t access account email

If you can’t access your account email, refer below, depending on what applies to you: You’re still logged in (or can still log in) to Canva You can change your registered email address now from the Account Settings. Go to your Account Settings, and update your account email to one that you can access. It’s a work email and you’ve left the company Try reaching out to your employer or email service provider for temporary access. Log in to your account, and change your account email to one that you have access to.

Not receiving account related emails

If you’re not receiving account-related emails (e.g. password reset, verification, etc.), here’s what you can do: Double-check if you entered or registered the correct email address. Check your email’s spam or Promotions (for Gmail) folders. If you found the email in these folders, mark them as “not spam” to make sure you receive future emails in your inbox. Refresh your email inbox as there can be delays in receiving emails sometimes. Resend emails . You can reset your password again or resend the verification email from the Account Settings . Check with your I.T. or email provider if emails from Canva are blocked. Depending on how strict your emails are set up, it might be possible that receiving emails outside of your organization is not allowed. If you still need help after doing the steps above, click here to contact our Support Team.

Change password

Secure your account by regularly changing your password. If you’ve forgotten your password, reset it here instead. Change your password now from the Account Settings. Here’s how to change your password: From the top corner of the homepage, click the ⚙ gear icon to go to your Account Settings. Click the Login & Security tab. Next to Password , click Update . Enter your current password for verification purposes. Enter your new password. Try making it at least 8 characters long. See this article for tips on picking a strong password. Re-enter it under Confirm new password . Click Save changes . A password change confirmation will be sent to your account email .
